Ship it

To put this on your glasses, open the builder from your Brain — the Mac panel → Plugins → “Build a lens” — and hit Deploy for one-click install. No Brain yet? Download the lens above; you can double-check it any time with dreamlayer figment safety my-lens.json.

Share it

A whole lens fits in a link (and a QR). Anyone can open it, remix it, and their glasses re-prove it's safe — so sharing carries zero risk.

Publish to the store →

Free to publish. The registry re-checks the proof and a maintainer signs it in — your listing's claims are recomputed, never trusted. A submission is a request, not a publish.
