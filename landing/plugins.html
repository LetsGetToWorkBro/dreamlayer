Every behavior proves it can't hurt you

Behaviors are data, not code — statically verified. Before you install one, your own device re-checks the proof and shows you exactly what it cannot do. Not "promises not to." Cannot.

Sear — a searing-station timer
This behavior CANNOT
  • pulse faster than 4 Hz — the photic-safety cap
  • talk back faster than 1 / s
  • show more than 5 lines at once
  • reach the network, files, camera, or microphone
  • swallow your kill switch
This behavior WILL
  • run 2 scenes, each held at least half a second
